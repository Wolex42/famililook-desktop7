## HOW TO USE THIS FILE

1. Start with **Mama Bear** (prompt 1). She sets the style anchor for all other characters.
2. Once you have a Mama Bear you like, generate the rest of the family in the same session/thread so DALL-E maintains style consistency.
3. Each prompt is self-contained — paste the entire block.
4. After generating, if a character drifts from the style, add: "Match the exact same art style as the previous character — same outline weight, same eye style, same proportions."
5. Generate each at **1024x1024** on a **white background**.
6. **STYLE TARGET**: Match the warmth, expressiveness, and detail level of the African family characters already generated. These characters should feel like they belong in the same app — same illustration quality, just different ethnicity and colour palette.

## PROMPT 1: MAMA BEAR — `mama_proud` (Generate First — Style Anchor)

```
Create a single cartoon character illustration on a plain white background. This character is called "Mama Bear" and she is the mother figure in a White/Caucasian family of illustrated mascots for a baby resemblance app called FamiliLook. She will be printed on ceramic mugs alongside real family photos, so she needs to feel warm, approachable, and gift-worthy — like something a new mum would proudly display on her desk.

IMPORTANT STYLE REFERENCE: This character must match the same illustration quality and expressiveness as an existing African family version of this character (warm, detailed, expressive cartoon with personality). NOT a flat geometric mascot — she should feel like a real character with personality and warmth.

STYLE RULES (apply to this and ALL future characters in this family):
- Art style: expressive, contemporary cartoon with rounded shapes and big personality. Think Duolingo's owl meets children's book illustration — warm, inviting, characterful.
- Rich, warm colour fills with subtle depth. NOT flat geometric shapes — she should feel alive and personable.
- Thick, consistent dark outlines around every shape with rounded endpoints and joins.
- Background is pure white with nothing else — no ground line, no shadow, no decorative elements, no environment.

MAMA BEAR ANATOMY:
- HEAD: A wide, rounded face with warm peach-fair skin tone (#F5C5A3). Rosy, warm complexion. The head is approximately 40% of the total character height.
- SKIN TONE: Warm peach (#F5C5A3) throughout — head, arms, legs. This is her natural fair skin colour with a warm undertone.
- HAIR: A beautiful rounded bun sitting on top of the head, with volume and personality. Rich auburn/chestnut brown (#5A2035) with 2-3 curved interior lines suggesting texture and flow. About 35% the diameter of the head. The bun should feel full and styled, not a simple circle.
- HAIR DETAIL: A few wisps or side-swept fringe visible at the forehead — giving her personality. Not just a bald head with a bun on top.
- EYES: Large, expressive brown eyes (#3D1F0E) with prominent white highlights that give them life and sparkle. Warm, kind expression. The eyes should be the emotional centre of the face.
- LASHES: Above each eye, two short thick curved strokes sweeping upward and outward. These are the key feature that distinguishes Mama from Papa. Same dark plum colour (#5A2035) as the outlines.
- MOUTH: A warm, confident smile — slightly open, showing genuine warmth. Not just a curved line — she should look like she's genuinely happy. Dark plum stroke.
- CHEEKS: Two soft rosy blush circles (#E8637A) at about 40% opacity, giving her a warm, healthy glow.
- BODY: A pear/bell shape — narrow at the top, widening to a broad rounded bottom. About 55% of total character height.
- DRESS: A beautiful rose pink dress (#C0364E) with a subtle pattern — 2-3 small lighter pink (#E8637A) hearts or polka dots as decoration. The dress should feel like real clothing with a neckline and hem detail, not just a coloured body shape. A thin waistband or belt detail in dark plum (#5A2035) to add structure.
- ARMS: Rounded, warm peach skin showing. They end in soft rounded hands (mitten style but with a thumb suggestion). The RIGHT arm (from our perspective) is bent with the hand resting on the hip. The LEFT arm is extended forward, holding a tea cup.
- LEGS: Short, visible below the dress hem. Simple rounded feet with small rose-coloured shoes (#E8637A).
- TEA CUP: A cute ceramic mug held in the left hand. Small rounded rectangle with a C-shaped handle. Two small wavy steam lines rising from the top. Mug fill: soft pink (#E8637A) with a tiny heart on it (#C0364E). Steam lines: light mauve (#C9A0AC).

POSE: Standing tall and confident. Body angled very slightly to the right (about 5-10 degrees). Head facing the viewer. She looks proud, warm, and self-assured — like a mum who just found out her baby looks exactly like her. Real personality in the pose, not stiff.

COLOUR PALETTE (exact hex values):
- Skin: #F5C5A3 (warm peach)
- Hair/bun: #5A2035 (rich auburn/chestnut)
- All outlines: #5A2035 (dark plum), consistent thickness
- Eye colour: #3D1F0E (warm brown) with #FFFFFF highlights
- Lash strokes: #5A2035
- Mouth: #5A2035
- Cheek blush: #E8637A at 40% opacity
- Dress: #C0364E (rose pink)
- Dress pattern: #E8637A (soft pink hearts/dots)
- Belt/waistband: #5A2035
- Shoes: #E8637A
- Tea cup: #E8637A with #C0364E heart
- Steam: #C9A0AC

SIZE AND COMPOSITION:
- The character fills about 70-75% of the square canvas vertically, centred horizontally.
- Clear breathing room above the bun and below the feet.
- The character should feel balanced, warm, and alive — not stiff or geometric.

DO NOT INCLUDE: Any text, any background elements, any ground/floor, any shadow beneath the character, any gradient on the background, any environment, any other characters. Just the single character on white.
```

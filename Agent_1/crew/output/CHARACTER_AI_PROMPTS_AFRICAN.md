## CULTURAL DESIGN NOTES

The African family variant uses cultural signifiers that are recognisable and respectful:
- **Mama**: Afro puff bun with gold headwrap accent, ankara-pattern wrap dress
- **Papa**: Clean fade haircut, neat beard, dashiki with embroidered neckline
- **Little Cub**: Small afro puff with gold bow, simple onesie
- **Mini Me**: Playful afro, casual modern clothing (universal kid energy)
- **Gran**: Full gele headwrap (most distinctive feature), kaftan/boubou
- **Gramps**: Kufi cap, grey goatee, agbada/grand boubou

The teal (#1A8B7A) and gold (#D4A54A) palette is inspired by West African textile traditions while maintaining the flat-cartoon aesthetic. These colours pair beautifully with the warm brown skin tones and create strong contrast on white backgrounds and ceramic mugs.

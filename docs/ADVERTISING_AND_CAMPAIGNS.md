## 3. Influencer Strategy

### Tier 1: Micro-Influencers (1K-50K followers) — Primary Focus

| Audience | Platform | Product | Budget per post | Volume |
|----------|----------|---------|----------------|--------|
| Parenting creators | Instagram, TikTok | FamiliLook | $100-300 | 10-20/month |
| Couples content | TikTok | FamiliMatch | $100-300 | 10-20/month |
| Family lifestyle | YouTube | Keepsakes | $200-500 | 5-10/month |

**Why micro**: Higher engagement rates (4-8% vs 1-3% for macro), authentic feel, lower cost. A parenting creator saying "we tried this" is more compelling than a celebrity ad.

## 4. Viral Mechanics (Built-In Growth)

### FamiliLook
- **Result sharing**: "Look who [child] looks like!" screenshot → social media
- **Keepsake photos**: Customers post received mugs/prints → organic UGC
- **Family challenge**: "Does your whole family agree? Run the analysis!"

### FamiliMatch
- **Face fusion sharing**: "Look what we'd look like combined" → inherently shareable
- **Group rooms**: 1 person invites 5 friends → each invites 5 more (network effect)
- **Compatibility challenge**: "We scored 92%" → friends want to beat it

### FamiliPoker
- **Game night sharing**: "Beat your Dad at Feature Poker" → family competitive moments
- **Tournament results**: Leaderboard screenshots → community building

### Family Mug Set
- **Unboxing content**: Physical product arrival is natural content
- **Gift reveal**: "We gave Mum her mug and she cried" → emotional virality
- **Kitchen display**: Mugs on shelf → passive brand exposure to every visitor

## 7. Advertising Compliance

### Platform-Specific Restrictions

| Platform | Restriction | How We Comply |
|----------|------------|---------------|
| Apple | No gambling promotion in non-gambling apps | FamiliPoker is "card games" not "gambling"; no cross-promo from FamiliLook |
| Facebook/Meta | Facial recognition ads restricted | Frame as "compare features" not "scan your face" |
| TikTok | Strict on dating app ads for under-18 | Age-gate FamiliMatch ads; use "compatibility" not "dating" |
| Google | Biometric data collection requires disclosure | Ad copy mentions "facial feature comparison" |
| ASA (UK) | Advertising must be truthful | No "100% accurate" claims; use "fun" and "entertainment" framing |

### Copy Guidelines

**DO say:**
- "See which parent your child looks like"
- "Compare your facial features"
- "How compatible are your faces?"
- "Fun family entertainment"
- "Personalised keepsake gifts"

**DO NOT say:**
- "We scan your face" (triggers biometric ad restrictions)
- "AI determines your child's genetics" (inaccurate, misleading)
- "100% accurate analysis" (entertainment product, not medical)
- "Never leaves your device" (photos are processed server-side — T-11)
- "Casino" in any FamiliPoker context (App Store risk)
